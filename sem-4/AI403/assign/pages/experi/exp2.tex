\subsection{Observation}
From these graphs and charts, we can deduce that this batch has an approximately equal ratio of males and females. Both groups have fairly similar average marks, study hour and attedance. Looking at the grade-wise distribution, we can see that as the grade descreases the study hours and attendance have also reduced.
